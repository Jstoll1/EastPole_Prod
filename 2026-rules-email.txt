RULES

*Select 10 golfers from the tiered pool below.

*Best four scores will be used as your team's foursome; lowest total score to par wins

*No more than five entries/person

*$20 buy-in/entry; fifth entry is $10

PAYOUTS

Top three get payouts:

3rd place – all entry fees reimbursed
2nd place – 30% of pot, less 3rd place finisher entry fees
1st place – 70% of post, less 3rd place finisher entry fees

TIEBREAKER

Should two or more teams in the top three have the same aggregate low score, the team's fifth-best score will be used. If the fifth score doesn't break the tie, we will use the sixth. And if tied after six scores, we will pay out evenly among the tied teams. (This applies only to teams in the top three.)
