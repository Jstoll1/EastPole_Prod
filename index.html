Pool Rules

The quick version — full email went out before tee-off.

Rules
  • Pick 10 golfers from the tiered pool
  • Best 4 scores count toward your team's foursome — lowest total to par wins
  • Up to 5 entries per person
  • $20 per entry · 5th entry is $10
Payouts (top 3)
  • 1st — 70% of pot (less 3rd-place entry-fee reimbursement)
  • 2nd — 30% of pot (less 3rd-place entry-fee reimbursement)
  • 3rd — all entry fees reimbursed
Tiebreaker (top 3 only)
  • 5th-best score breaks the tie
  • Then 6th-best if still tied
  • If still tied after six scores, payout splits evenly among the tied teams
